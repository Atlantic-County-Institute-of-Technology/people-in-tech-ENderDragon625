The Life of Paul Allen:

Paul Allen was born January 21, 1953 in Seattle to Ken and Faye Allen. Allen was always fascinated with science, starting his own science club in his basement when he was 10 years old. Paul attended Lakeside School where he gained a newfound passion for computer science. His love for computers was shared by another student who went by the name Bill Gates. The two quickly bonded and became very good friends and eventually started to work on what would turn out be one of the most life changing creations, Microsoft.
